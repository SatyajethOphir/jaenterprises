Privacy Policy — JAEnterprise (Offline Store)

At JAEnterprise, which includes JA Stationery and Nuthan Medical, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you provide when visiting or purchasing at our offline store.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic Contact Information: Name, phone number, or email (for billing, loyalty programs, or order support)
  • Purchase Details: Items purchased, quantity, price, payment method
  • Medical Records: Prescription details as required by law
  • Surveillance: CCTV footage for safety and security

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and track orders or returns
  • To provide invoices and customer support
  • For compliance (especially with medical regulations)
  • To improve customer service and product availability
  • To maintain store security

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share your data only:

  • With health professionals (for prescription validation)
  • With government bodies when legally required
  • With billing/service providers under confidentiality

4. CCTV Surveillance

Our store is monitored via CCTV for safety. Recordings are:

  • Stored securely
  • Accessed only by authorized staff
  • Kept temporarily unless needed for investigation

5. Customer Rights

As a customer, you can:

  • Request to see what data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletion where allowed
  • Opt out of promotional messages (if any)

6. Prescription Handling (Nuthan Medical)

  • Prescriptions are securely stored as per drug laws
  • Shared only with legal or authorized personnel

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep your data safe and accessible only to authorized staff.
