Who We Are

JANUZEN Enterprises is the parent company of Nuthan Medicals and JA Stationery. Our mission is to provide high-quality healthcare solutions and premium stationery products that empower communities, businesses, and institutions. With a commitment to trust, innovation, and integrity, we continue to build long-lasting relationships with customers across India.

Our Mission

To provide trusted healthcare and quality stationery solutions that enhance lives and learning.

Our Vision

To be a leader in healthcare and stationery, known for excellence, innovation, and social responsibility.

Our Values

Integrity, Trust, Innovation, Customer-Centricity, and Excellence guide everything we do.

Our History

Nuthan Medicals - Established 2005

Nuthan Medicals was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide high-quality healthcare and medical supplies to the community. Over the years, it has become a trusted name in pharmacies and medical distribution, known for reliability, customer care, and innovation in healthcare solutions.

JA Stationery - Established 2024

In 2024, JANUZEN Enterprises expanded into the world of education and office supplies by launching JA Stationery. Our mission is to deliver premium quality stationery products that inspire learning, creativity, and productivity in schools, offices, and homes across the region.
